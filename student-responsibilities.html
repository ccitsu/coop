Pre-Training Responsibilities

1. Register for Course

Register for the field training course online.

2. Attend Seminars

Attend all seminars and workshops on the program.

3. Prepare Resume

Write a complete resume and arrange for a training place.

4. Contact Advisor

Contact the assigned Faculty Advisor to discuss the program and communication methods.

5. Select Company

Select a coop company and get its approval.

6. Request Letter

Obtain Coop Request letter from Faculty Advisor (Form S1).

Responsibilities during Training

Submit Joining Notification

Submit the signed Coop Student Commencing Notification (Form S2) to the CCIT Training Unit and faculty advisor by the end of the first week.

Sign Pledge Form

Sign and submit the pledge form.

Upload Work Plan

Upload an e-copy of the work plan to your faculty advisor within the first two weeks.

Full Attendance

Attend the entire training program and report any problems to the Faculty Advisor.

Follow Organization Rules

Adhere to the organization's rules and regulations, remembering they are representing the university.

Monthly Reports

Submit monthly progress reports to the Faculty Advisor.

Evaluation Forms

Remind the Field Supervisor to fill out the evaluation forms.

Post-Training Responsibilities

Final Report Revisions

Make all necessary modifications to the final report as suggested by advisors and examiners.

Submit Final Report

Submit a detailed final report (both hard and soft copies) and a formal presentation about their work.

Present to Committee

Present their work to the Field Training Examining Committee on the scheduled date.

Complete Surveys

Fill out the online course exit and student exit surveys.

Important Reminders

  • Week 1: Submit signed Commencing Notification (Form S2)
  • Week 2: Upload approved work plan to Faculty Advisor
  • Monthly: Submit progress reports throughout training
  • End of Training: Ensure supervisor completes evaluation forms
  • Post-Training: Prepare and submit final report and presentation

Code of Ethics and Conduct

  • Remember that you are an ambassador of Shaqra University.
  • Ensure you have a training plan that is closely related to your academic major.
  • Keep an accurate daily/weekly record of your activities.
  • Do not wait until the end of the program to start writing your final report.
  • Maintain regular contact with your Faculty Advisor.
  • Take the initiative to gain useful experience; be proactive, not passive.
  • Respect the rules, regulations, and culture of the training organization.
  • Maintain confidentiality of any data or information you access during your training.
  • Demonstrate integrity, punctuality, and professionalism in all assigned tasks.
  • Treat colleagues and supervisors with respect, cooperation, and courtesy.
