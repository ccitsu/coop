Duration and Credit Hours

The duration of the CCIT field training program is 28 weeks, with a minimum of 6 daily working hours, which is equivalent to 10-credit hours. Students enrolled under the old plan will need to complete 6-8 weeks of training.

Enrollment Criteria

Students must meet the following criteria to be eligible for the field training program:

Department Permission

Obtain permission from their respective department.

Credit Hours Requirement

Have completed 110 credit hours of coursework, including all required core classes.

Full Availability Required

Students must be totally free during the training period. Taking any credit hours or courses concurrently with the field training is strictly not permitted.

Selection of Training Organizations

The CCIT Training Unit is responsible for directing students to suitable training organizations. Students may also suggest their own training location, subject to the approval of their department and the CCIT Training Unit. Training can take place at a selected organization inside or outside Saudi Arabia.

Cancellations or Alterations

Any request for cancellation or alteration of the training must be submitted to the CCIT Training Unit and will be handled according to university procedures. A student may request to change their training location within the first two weeks of the program if there are issues with the work plan or work ethics; otherwise, the request may be denied.

Important: Change requests must be submitted within the first two weeks of training. Requests made after this period may be denied.
