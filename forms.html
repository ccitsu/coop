Field (Coop) Training Final Report Template

The final report is a comprehensive document that reflects your learning experience during the field training. It should include:

Report Structure

  1. Title Page
    • Student name and ID
    • Training organization name
    • Training period (dates)
    • Faculty Advisor name
  2. Abstract/Executive Summary
    • Brief overview of the training experience (250-300 words)
  3. Introduction
    • About the training organization
    • Department/division where you trained
    • Your role and responsibilities
  4. Training Activities
    • Detailed description of tasks and projects
    • Technologies and tools used
    • Challenges faced and how you overcame them
  5. Learning Outcomes
    • Skills acquired
    • Knowledge gained
    • How classroom learning was applied
  6. Conclusion and Recommendations
    • Overall experience reflection
    • Career insights gained
    • Recommendations for future trainees
  7. References
    • Properly cited sources (if applicable)
  8. Appendices
    • Work samples, screenshots, code snippets (if allowed)
    • Training certificate
    • Signed evaluation forms
Formatting Guidelines:
  • Font: Times New Roman or Arial, 12pt
  • Spacing: 1.5 or double-spaced
  • Margins: 1 inch on all sides
  • Page numbers: Bottom center or top right
  • Minimum length: 15-20 pages (excluding appendices)
