Field Training Goals

By participating in the Field Training program, students are expected to:

Practical Work Experience

Gain practical work experience directly related to their major field of study.

Career Clarity

Clarify their academic and career goals.

Apply Learning

Apply and integrate classroom learning into a working environment.

Professional Understanding

Gain a deep understanding of professional cultures and expectations.

Professional Development

Develop professional habits, ethics, and self-confidence.

Field Training General Learning Outcomes

Upon completion of the program, students should be able to demonstrate the following abilities:

Knowledge Skills

  • Recognize professional and ethical responsibilities
  • Recognize the need for lifelong learning
  • Comprehend contemporary issues in the workplace

Cognitive Skills

  • Apply classroom knowledge to actual work situations
  • Evaluate and constructively apply professional feedback

Interpersonal Skills

  • Demonstrate the ability to work as part of a multi-disciplinary team
  • Resolve conflicts in a diverse environment

Communication, IT, and Numerical Skills

  • Exhibit effective communication skills
  • Employ job search and decision-making skills
  • Illustrate the ability to use relevant technology in the workplace
